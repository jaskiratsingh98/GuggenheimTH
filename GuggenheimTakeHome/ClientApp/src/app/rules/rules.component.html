The total cost of a NYC taxi trip is calculated using the following formula:

  • Base Fee: Each trip has a $3.00 charge upon entry
  • Peak Hour Weekday Surcharge: A fee of #1.00 is added on weekdays between the time of 4:00 PM and 8:00 PM
  • Night Surcharge: A fee of $0.50 is added after 8:00 PM & before 6:00 AM
  • Unit Price: $0.35 are charged for each additional unit where the unit is defined as:
    • one-fifth of a mile, when the taxicab is traveling at less than 6 miles an hour
    • 60 seconds when not in motion or traveling at 6 miles per hour or more
  • New York State Tax Surcharge: $0.50 is added for each ride
